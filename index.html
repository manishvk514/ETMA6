04 / method

How we work.

01

We start with the feeling.

Before references, before tools. What should this make someone feel. Everything after serves that answer.

02

We build the world.

Palette, lens, light, texture. The visual universe the work will live in. Decided on paper, before the first frame gets made.

03

We compose every shot.

Every frame is a decision. The same care a cinematographer brings to a set.

04

We direct the motion.

Pace, pause, the weight of a beat. What moves, and what doesn't. Motion is how a picture becomes a feeling.

05

We finish when it's right.

Color, sound, the final cut. Done is when the work stops asking for changes — not when the calendar asks for delivery.

The best work feels like someone couldn't stop thinking about it. Work you'd watch twice.

— that's the bar we're working to.
